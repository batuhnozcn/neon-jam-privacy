Privacy Policy for Neon Jam

Last updated: June 9, 2026

The Neon Jam Team ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Neon Jam mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

We prioritize your privacy. The App operates primarily locally on your device. We do not directly collect, store, or process personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, or email) on our servers. Game progress, unlocked items, and virtual currency are stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising

To support the development of the App, we use third-party advertising services, specifically Google AdMob. These third-party services may collect and use certain information to provide personalized advertisements.

  • Device Identifiers: AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (e.g., IDFA on iOS) and other device-level information to serve targeted ads.
  • Cookies & Tracking: These services may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to analyze ad performance and user interaction.

For more detailed information on how Google uses data, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

3. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

In compliance with Apple's guidelines, if you are using iOS 14.5 or later, we will present the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. This requests your explicit permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites for targeted advertising purposes. You have full control over this and can change your preference at any time via your device's Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

4. In-App Purchases (StoreKit)

The App offers virtual goods for purchase (In-App Purchases). All payment transactions are securely processed entirely by Apple via the App Store (StoreKit). We do not have access to, nor do we collect or store, your credit card information, billing details, or any financial data. Your purchase history is managed by your Apple ID.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. The advertisements served by third-party networks may process data based on their respective policies. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we will be able to take necessary actions.

6. Data Security

While no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any data associated with our App. Since game data is stored locally, the security of this data is tied to the security of your device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
